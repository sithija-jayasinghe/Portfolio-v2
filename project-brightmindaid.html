The Problem

Access to quality educational resources in Sri Lanka is often fragmented. Students struggle to find past papers, study notes, and textbooks in one central, reliable location. During times of crisis or disaster, this gap becomes even more critical when physical access to schools is limited.

The Tech Stack

React
Vite
Supabase
Tailwind

Key Challenges

  • Optimization for Low Bandwidth: Ensuring the platform loaded quickly even for students with poor internet connections required aggressive lazy loading and efficient asset management.
  • Scalable File Storage: Managing uploads of large PDF textbooks and past papers required a robust storage solution, which I implemented using Supabase Storage buckets with strict RLS policies.
  • Community Moderation: Since the platform is community-driven, implementing a vetting system for uploaded content was crucial to maintain quality.

The Result

A responsive, PWA-enabled web platform that allows students to freely access and share educational materials. The platform includes smart search, filtering by subject/grade, and offline capabilities.
